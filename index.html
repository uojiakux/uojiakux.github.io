About Me

Hello Everyone! Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Ugonna Ojiaku, and I am one of the students of the UMN Twin Cities Coding Bootcamp. I am happy to be here! I am enjoying my time and learning so far. I come from a family of four, and I am the youngest child. I currently live in Brooklyn Park next to my sister and parents. My brother lives in Atlanta, Georgia and my other sister lives in San Mateo, California. Family means a lot to me! I am thankful to have them, as they inspire much of the decisions I make in life. With that being said, my dad is a software engineer and is ecstatic I'm in this program. Everyone has been supportive, but he is definitely front runner. That is a little bit about me. Feel free to look at the rest of my site and portfolio. I am still pretty new to web developing, but if you see anything you like, please contact me. I would love to discuss ideas, projects and/or hot topics. Happy browsing!
